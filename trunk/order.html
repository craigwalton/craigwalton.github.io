Installation

  1. Wrap rubber material tightly around pole.
  2. Bring Stair Hook and best-fitting Clasp together, around the rubber.
  3. Use a phillips screwdriver to tighten the bolts. Should be hand tight (do not over-tighten).

Product Specification

Stair Hook is designed to fix onto vertical poles of diameter 20mm-30mm which is approximately the diameter of a 1 pence and 2 pound coin. It is designed to support bikes under the saddle. It could be used to support bikes by the frame, but brake and gear cables may get in the way.

Safety

Stair Hook has been designed to support bikes up to a maximum of 20kg. Please check the balusters of your staircase are structurally sound before employing Stair Hook. Make sure that the seat post is securely fastened to the bike frame. For added safety, always lock your bike with a D-lock (or U-lock) to the staircase as well as suspending it from the saddle on Stair Hook.
